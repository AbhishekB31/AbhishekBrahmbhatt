Key Features


  • Jump and Run Mechanics: Players navigate through vibrant, side-scrolling worlds filled with obstacles, using Mario's jumping and running abilities to overcome challenges.
  • Diverse Enemies: Encounter two types of enemies, including the mischievous Goombas and the menacing Koopa Troopas. Defeat or evade them to progress.
  • Underground Level: Explore the depths of the Mushroom Kingdom with a thrilling underground level, featuring its own unique challenges and surprises.
  • Collect power-ups to enhance Mario's abilities, including the iconic Super Mushroom to grow bigger and the Starman for temporary invincibility.
  • Finishing Flag: Reach the end of each level and celebrate victory by grabbing the iconic finishing flagpole.
  • Castle Transitions: Venture into mysterious castles that serve as gateways to new and exciting maps, keeping the gameplay fresh and engaging.
  • Permadeath Challenge: Test your skills with the game's permadeath feature - if you die, the game restarts, offering a true test of your platforming abilities.
Super Mario Bros captures the essence of classic gaming while showcasing my passion for game design. It's a must-play experience for anyone seeking a nostalgic journey through the Mushroom Kingdom.

Summary

I created a captivating and nostalgic game inspired by the iconic Super Mario Bros. In this exciting adventure, players journey through a colorful world filled with challenging obstacles and quirky characters, just like the classic game that inspired it. With meticulous attention to detail and a deep appreciation for gaming history, I crafted an homage that captures the spirit of the original while offering a fresh and engaging experience for players of all ages. This project reflects my passion for game design and my dedication to preserving the magic of timeless classics in the world of gaming.
